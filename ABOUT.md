1.1 About Terralume 
Terralume Ltd is a buyer-side real estate advisory firm based in Lagos, Nigeria. Terralume 
works exclusively for property buyers and renters — never for sellers, landlords, or developers. 
The firm sources, verifies, conducts due diligence, and negotiates property transactions entirely 
in the interest of the client. 
The website serves as the primary digital presence for Terralume, functioning simultaneously as 
a marketing platform, a trust-building tool, a client acquisition system, and an operational 
resource for existing clients and staff. 
 
1.2 Website Purpose & Goals 
Primary goals of the Terralume website: 
• Generate qualified leads from diaspora buyers, HNW individuals, first-time buyers, 
and corporate HR managers. 
• Build institutional trust and credibility in a market where trust is scarce. 
• Educate the Lagos property market about buyer-side advisory and Terralume's 
differentiated model. 
• Enable existing clients to track their engagement and communicate with their Project 
Manager. 
• Support the Terralume team with operational tools and document management. 
• Establish Terralume as the authoritative voice on Lagos real estate intelligence.
